Hello world of grid!

Here we are looking at the grid as a way to create webpages that dynamically change with screen-size.

The method: Grid columns

In the CSS, there is a simple media query with a chosen screen-width. If the screen is Maximum n, the boxes are stacked one way (underneath each other); if it is Minimum n+1, the boxes are side-by-side. (More or less.)

This is a simple way to create a mobile friendly page.

Mozilla and w3schools have some different takes on this. Mozilla has just updated theirs, but I still suggest you look at both and decide which makes most sense for you.
