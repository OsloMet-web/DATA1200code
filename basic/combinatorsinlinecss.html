Presenting a list where different levels have slight differences in fonts

Below, I have four levels of Unordered List. The code, simplified looks like this:

            <UL> (1st Level, parent)
                <li></li>
                <UL> (2nd Level, child)
                    <li></li>
                    <UL> (3rd Level, grand-child)
                        <li></li>
                        <UL>  (4th Level, 'any decendant', this is grand-child of L2 as well*)
                            <li></li>
                        </ul>
                    </ul>
                </ul>
            </ul>
(* the L4, that is both an "any" descendant of L1, grand-child of L2 and child of L3 complicates things. But without switching between UL and OL, I found no simple work-around. You will have to accept that this much nesting will always be slightly complex.)
